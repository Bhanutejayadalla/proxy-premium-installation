## 1. Authentication

### Register
1. Open the app → Tap **"Don't have an account? Register"** at the bottom.
2. Enter a **username**, **email**, and **password**.
3. Tap **Register** → The account is created and you're logged in automatically.

### Login
1. Enter your **email** and **password**.
2. Tap **Login**.
3. The app remembers your last email for quick re-login.

### Logout
1. Go to the **Profile** tab (bottom-right icon).
2. Tap the **logout** button in the app bar.
3. You'll be returned to the login screen.

## 2. Dual Mode (Pro / Social)

Proxi Premium has two modes — **Pro** (formal/professional) and **Social** (casual).

### Switching Modes
1. Tap the **grid icon** (⊞) in the top-right corner of the home screen.
2. The mode toggles between Pro and Social.
3. Your feed, connections, and chat all switch to the selected mode's content.

### What Changes Per Mode
| Feature | Pro Mode | Social Mode |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| Feed | Professional posts | Casual/social posts |
| Avatar | Formal avatar shown | Casual avatar shown |
| Connections | Pro connections only | Social connections only |
| Chat | Pro conversations | Social conversations |

## 3. Home Feed

### Viewing Posts
1. Open the **Home** tab (house icon, bottom-left).
2. Scroll through posts from connected users.
3. Stories appear at the top in circular avatars — tap to view.

### Interacting with Posts
- **Like**: Tap the heart icon below a post.
- **Comment**: Tap the comment icon → type your comment → tap send.
- **View author profile**: Tap the username or avatar.

### Visibility Rules
- **Public** posts are visible to everyone.
- **Connections-only** posts appear only for your connections.
- **Private** posts are visible only to the author.

## 4. Nearby Discovery

### Opening Nearby
1. Tap the **Nearby** tab (globe icon, second from left).

### Choosing Discovery Mode
- **Bluetooth** — Works **fully offline**. Uses BLE to detect Proxi users within ~30-50 meters.
- **GPS** — Works **online**. Uses GPS + Firestore to find users within a 10 km radius.

Tap the **Bluetooth** or **GPS** chip at the top to switch modes.

### Scanning
1. Tap the **radar/GPS icon** in the center of the screen.
2. Wait for the scan to complete:
   - BLE mode: ~12 seconds (8s scan + processing)
   - GPS mode: ~4 seconds
3. Discovered users appear in a list below.

### Interacting with Discovered Users
- **Chat**: Tap the message icon (💬) next to a user.
- **Connect**: Tap the add-user icon (➕) to send a connection request.
- **View Profile**: Tap anywhere on the user row.
- **Status badges**: "Connected" (green), "Pending" (orange), "Accept?" (blue).

## 5. Create Post / Story / Reel

### Creating a Post
1. Tap the **Post** tab (+ icon, center of bottom bar).
2. Type your text in the text field.
3. Optionally attach a photo by tapping the image icon.
4. Tap **Share** in the top-right corner.

### Creating a Story
1. Same as creating a post, but toggle the **"Story"** switch before sharing.
2. Stories expire after 24 hours.

### Creating a Reel
1. From the Home feed, tap the **Reels** section.
2. Pick a video file.
3. Add a caption and tap Share.

## 7. Chat (Direct Messages)

### Opening Chats
1. Tap the **Chat** tab (speech bubble icon) in the bottom bar.
2. The **Direct** tab shows all your 1-on-1 conversations.

### Starting a Chat
- **From Nearby**: After scanning, tap the chat icon next to a discovered user.
- **From Profile**: Open any user's profile → tap the **Message** button.
- **From Connections**: Open your connections list → tap a connected user.

### Sending Messages
1. Open a conversation.
2. Type in the text field at the bottom.
3. Tap the **send** icon (→).

### Sending Photos
1. Tap the **attach** icon (📎) at the bottom-left.
2. Select an image from your gallery.
3. The image is uploaded and sent automatically.

### Managing Messages
- **Delete a message**: Long-press your own message → confirm deletion.
- **Clear all messages**: Tap ⋮ (menu) → "Clear Messages".
- **Delete conversation**: Tap ⋮ (menu) → "Delete Conversation".

## 8. Group Chat

### Creating a Group
1. Go to **Chat** tab → **Groups** tab.
2. Tap the **users icon** in the top-right.
3. Enter a group name and select members from your connections.
4. Tap **Create**.

### Group Messaging
- Same as direct messages — type, send, attach images.
- Messages show the sender's username above each bubble.

### Managing Groups
- **Clear messages**: ⋮ → "Clear Messages"
- **Delete group**: ⋮ → "Delete Group"

## 9. Connections

### Viewing Connections
1. From the **Profile** tab, tap **Connections** or the connections count.

## 10. Connection Requests

### Viewing Requests
1. Go to **Connections** screen → tap **Requests** in the top-right.
2. See all pending connection requests (from any mode).

### Accepting a Request
1. Tap **Accept** (✓) next to a pending request.

1. Tap **Decline** (✗) next to a pending request.

## 11. Profile Management

### Viewing Your Profile
1. Tap the **Profile** tab (person icon, bottom-right).
2. See your username, full name, bio, headline, followers, following.

### Editing Your Profile
1. On the Profile tab, tap the **Edit** (pencil) icon.
2. Update: Full Name, Username, Bio, Headline, Visibility setting.
3. Tap **Save**.

### Changing Avatar
1. In Edit Profile, tap the camera icon on your avatar.
2. Choose a photo from your gallery.
3. The avatar uploads automatically and updates in real-time.
4. You can set separate avatars for Pro and Social modes.

### Profile Visibility
- **Public**: Anyone can see your posts and profile.
- **Connections**: Only your connections can see your content.
- **Private**: Only you can see your content.

## 15. Bluetooth (BLE) Offline Discovery

### How It Works
- When you log in, Proxi Premium starts **BLE advertising** your user ID.
- Other Proxi users scanning nearby will detect your signal.
- **No internet connection needed** — discovery works via Bluetooth only.
- Range: approximately **30-50 meters** in open space.

### Requirements
- **Bluetooth must be turned ON** on your device.
- Grant **Bluetooth** (Scan, Advertise, Connect) and **Location** permissions when prompted.

### Troubleshooting BLE
| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| "Bluetooth is turned off" | Enable Bluetooth in device Settings |
| "Permissions denied" | Grant all Bluetooth + Location permissions in Settings |
| No users found | Ensure the other person also has Proxi Premium open with BLE mode |
| Chip shows "Off" | Turn on Bluetooth in your device settings, then scan again |

## 16. GPS Online Discovery

### How It Works
- Uses your phone's GPS to get your location.
- Uploads your coordinates to Firestore.
- Finds other Proxi users within a **10 km radius**.
- **Requires internet connection**.

### Requirements
- **Location services** must be enabled.
- **Internet connection** (WiFi or mobile data).
- Grant **Location** permission when prompted.

### Troubleshooting GPS
| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| "Could not get location" | Enable Location services in device Settings |
| "Requires internet" | Connect to WiFi or mobile data |
| No users found | Other users must be online and within 10 km |

## Quick Tips

- **Switch modes frequently** to see both professional and social content.
- **Sync your cache** while online so BLE discovery can show user profiles offline.
- **Keep Bluetooth and Location on** for the best nearby discovery experience.
- **Set separate avatars** for Pro and Social modes to maintain different personas.
- **Pull down to refresh** on any feed screen to get the latest content.
